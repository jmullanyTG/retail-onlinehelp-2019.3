Content Update window

When Save is clicked to save the updated Promotion Content and the Promotion has been placed on a future Event or the product in the Promotion has Master Content, the Content Update window is displayed.

In the window, you can choose to push Promotion Content (i.e. Copy Text, Images or both) to the following:

  • Master Content – If the Promotion consists of a single Product or Product Group that exists in Content Manager, it will be available to update with the Promotion Content.
  • Event Content – If the Promotion is added to any future Events, they will be listed and you can select which Events, if any, should be updated with the Promotion Content.

Click Ok to save and push changes.
