Site cluster validation statuses

The first column allows you to see at a glance the Validation Status of the Promotion for each Site Cluster. The possible Statuses are (most severe to least):

  • Red no entry – An error was found so you must review the Promotion to resolve the problem.
  • Orange exclamation – A Validation warning was encountered.
  • Green ‘i’ – Validation ran successfully but additional information is available to review.
  • Red exclamation – A Calculation is required before a Validation Status can be assigned, so you must either manually open the Promotion and recalculate, or it can be recalculated by an overnight process.
  • Green checkmark – The Promotion is successfully validated.

The most severe Validation Status at the Site Cluster level will roll up to display the Validation Status at the Promotion level.
