Promotions screen

The Promotion screen has three tabs for you to select from: List, Promotion Calendar, and Variant/Item Master Calendar. Additional tabs display when you double-click individual Promotions.

Changing multiple Promotion statuses at once

You can change the status of multiple Promotions at once thus avoiding having to open and change the status for each and every Promotion. To change multiple statuses at once, select the Change Status(es) button.

Use the Change Statuses dialog to select the statuses to be changed.
