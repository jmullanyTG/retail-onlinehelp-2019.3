Buy get type Promotion Strategies

When selecting a Buy Get Type strategy the Create New Promotion will look different than when selecting a Promoted Price/Promoted Discount type strategies. Instead of adding products/groups to the Promotion Group you will need to add products/groups to the Buy Group and Get Group.

Click on Edit which will display the Edit Buy Get Group window:

  • Edit the Buy Group

    1. Highlight the record against the trolley icon of the Buy Group criteria and click on Edit and the Edit Buy Group window will be displayed.
    2. Add and remove products/groups in the same manner as when adding products/groups for the Promoted Discount and Promoted Promotion Strategies, by clicking on Add Item/Add Variant or Add Item Group/Add Variant Group and enter the Buy Quantity and any other information required for the Buy Group.
    3. Click OK then click Close.

  • Edit the Get Group

    1. Highlight the record against the handbag icon of the Get Group criteria and click on Edit.
    2. The Edit Get Group Screen will open.
    3. Add and remove products/groups in the same manner as when adding products/groups for the Promoted Discount and Promoted Price strategies, by clicking on Add Item/Add Variant or Add Item Group/Add Variant Group and enter the Buy Quantity and any other information required for the Get Group.
    4. Click OK then click Close.

After the Buy and Get Groups have been set up, click Close. The products/groups chosen will be displayed under the Promotion Group \ Buy Group or Promotion Group \ Get Group respectively. The pricing information can be entered after the Promotion is saved.
