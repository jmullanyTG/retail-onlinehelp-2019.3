Delete a Promotion

You may delete a Promotion if you have the appropriate rights to do so for the current Promotion Status. Once a Promotion is deleted, all Promotion level Site Clusters, product groups, filters, pricing, copy and images linked to the Promotion, will also be permanently deleted.

There are four ways to delete a Promotion:

  • Promotions Home Screen – List
    1. From the List tab, select the check box next to the Promotion you wish to delete.
    2. Click on Delete.
    3. Click Yes to delete the Promotion.
  • Promotions Home Screen – Promotion Calendar
    1. From the Promotion Calendar tab, select the Promotion you wish to delete.
    2. Click on Delete.
    3. Click Yes to delete the Promotion.
  • Promotions Home Screen – Item / Variant Calendar
    1. From the Item / Variant Calendar tab, select the Promotion you wish to delete.
    2. Click on Delete.
    3. Click Yes to delete the Promotion.
  • Promotion Detail
    1. From within the Promotion itself, click on Delete at the top of the screen.
    2. Click Yes to delete the Promotion.
