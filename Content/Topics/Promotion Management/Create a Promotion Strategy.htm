Create a Promotion Strategy

Within the Promotion Strategy screen, click New to create a new Promotion Strategy. Clicking New adds a new blank row at the end of the rows in the Promotion Strategies grid. Complete the fields as required in the Promotion Strategies pane and then select the check boxes as required in the Promotion Strategy Field Requirements pane.

Removing access from Roles and Users to a new Promotion Strategy

By default, all Roles and Users have access to all new Promotion Strategies. To maintain corporate compliance across advertising channels, click on the Edit User Access field to restrict access to Roles and Users as required (Moving a Role or User to the Remove Role\User column does not affect any current Promotions). Once Roles and Uses have been selected, click Save.

Only active Roles and Users that have User Right Allow Creation of Promotion are displayed in the Remove User Rights dialog.
