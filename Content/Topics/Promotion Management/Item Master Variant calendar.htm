Item Master/Variant calendar

The Item Master/Variant (i.e. depending on the environment configuration) Calendar displays a Gantt chart view of the Promotions loaded against products over time. Double clicking a Promotion, within the calendar, will open up the Promotion Detail of screen for review.

The Time Scale ‘+’ and ‘–‘ buttons can be used to view Promotions displayed by time of day, day, week, month, quarter or year. Filter can be used to filter the list of Promotions as in the List tab.
