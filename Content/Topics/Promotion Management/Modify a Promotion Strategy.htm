Modify a Promotion Strategy

Modifications can be made directly to the Promotion Strategy Configuration grid. Changes can also be made to the Promotion Strategy Field Requirements grid associated with that Promotion Strategy Configuration grid. Clicking Save above each of these grids will save any modifications to the associated grid. Click Cancel in the associated grid to discard any modifications.

Existing Promotions referencing this Promotion Strategy must either be recalculated manually or by an overnight process.
