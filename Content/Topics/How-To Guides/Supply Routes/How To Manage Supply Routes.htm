How To Manage Supply Routes

Demand Management provides centralized supply chain management, letting you control how your inventory moves from suppliers to distribution centers to stores. You can select primary and alternate routes of supply for each of your locations, specify delivery lead times, set ordering and delivery schedules, rank store priorities, and more. Demand Management does the rest, ensuring that its ordering and replenishment processes can help you get the right amount of inventory to the right stores at the right times.

This process guides you through the end-to-end process of managing supply routes for your sites. It starts with identifying newly added sites from your ERP system, takes you through creating supply chains for those sites, and ends with orders being generated for the new supply chains.
