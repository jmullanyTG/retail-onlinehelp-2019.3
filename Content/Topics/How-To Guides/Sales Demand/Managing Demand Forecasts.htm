Managing Demand Forecasts

Demand Management uses an item's sales history as a primary factor in forecasting future demand for the item. However, there may be instances when you want Demand Management to take other factors into consideration as well, such as:

  • Events and promotions which alter the usual sales for an item over a specific period of time.
  • Item phase-out dates, beyond which the item will no longer be sold.

You can manage events, promotions, and phase-out dates manually from within Demand Management.

Managing forecast exceptions

Because it is impractical to manually review thousands of item forecasts, Demand Management reviews forecasts automatically and alerts you if it discovers issues that may need human review. These issues (called exceptions) typically mean that something unusual or unexpected was discovered in a specific forecast, and someone with knowledge of the product should review the forecast exception to determine whether the forecast is accurate or should be altered.

Results
