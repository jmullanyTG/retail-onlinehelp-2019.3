Allocate Strategy DC Site Details tab

The Allocate Strategy DC Site Details tab will display stock holding information for the sites which have been designated as distribution centers as part of the setup configuration. This information can be used to help drive the formation of allocation strategies.

Users may view DC level details relating to their strategies.

Users are able to manage DC level weeks of cover values through input to the “WOC” field within this view and selecting “save”.

A detailed Item Master (size) level breakdown is also available as a read-only view.
