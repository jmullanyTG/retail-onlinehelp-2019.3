Row Total Options

  • Show Row Grand Totals – Will display the grand totals for rows which may be summed.
  • Show Row Totals – Will display the total for the row if the column may be summed.
  • Row Totals Editable – If checked, the row totals may be changed.
  • Row Grand Totals Editable – If checked, the row grand totals may be changed.
  • Row Custom Totals Editable – If checked, will make any custom row totals editable.
