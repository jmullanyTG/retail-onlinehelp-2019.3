Column Total Options

  • Show Column Grand Totals – Will display the grand totals for columns which may be summed.
  • Show Column Totals – Will display the total for the column if the column may be summed.
  • Column Totals Editable – If checked, the column totals may be changed.
  • Column Grand Totals Editable – If checked, the column grand totals may be changed.
  • Column Custom Totals Editable – If checked, will make any custom column totals editable.
