Widgets

Widgets are an extremely flexible and powerful means by which to display data within a Demand Management view. Easily the most customizable feature within the Demand Management software suite, the effective use of widgets is essential to a successful user experience. As the core work surfaces within Demand Management, widgets will display the information via data grids, pivot grids, graphs or schedules and provide users with opportunities to interact with relevant data in a straightforward and intuitive manner. One or more widgets will be contained in a particular view.
