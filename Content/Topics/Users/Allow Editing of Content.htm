Allow Editing of Content

Provides access to edit copy and images associated with the Promotion. Depending on the User Rights granted for Content Manager, you may also have access to update Events where the Promotion was placed and Master level Content for the product. The “Allow Access to Content” User Right must also be granted to display the Content button.

This permission may be configured to be enabled or disabled during specific Promotion Workflow Status’ States
