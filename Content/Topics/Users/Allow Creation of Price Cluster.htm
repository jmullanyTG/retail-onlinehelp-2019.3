Allow Creation of Price Cluster

Provides access to create Site Clusters in the Promotion, which allows you to specify a different Promotion Strategy or pricing at the Site Cluster level. The “Allow Editing of Price Cluster” User Right must also be granted to provide the ability to add and remove Sites to newly created Site Clusters.

This permission may be configured to be enabled or disabled during specific Promotion Workflow Status’ States
