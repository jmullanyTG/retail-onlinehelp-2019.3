Plan

Planning is comprised of the following two solutions, depending on your Demand Management license and user privileges.

Merchandise Financial Planning:  displays a plan approval workflow with an innovative approach to data management and user interaction. From this tab you can create, approve, and manage financial plans.

Assortment Planing: after your plan framework is created, fine tune at multiple levels and apply supplemental analysis from integrated supporting modules to craft your ideal buy strategy. From this tab, you can plan promotions, manage assortment plans, track your plan accuracy and make adjustments in-season.
