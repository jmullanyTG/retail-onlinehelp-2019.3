Product Changes panel

Suppressed as per DMDEV-62028. This functionality may be revived in future if any customer shows interest.

The Price Manager screen has a panel labeled Product Changes that displays a list of imported product changes that could not be applied to active Price Actions. The list is filtered by a user’s product permissions and will restrict selection, to take action upon, by month. Dismissing a product change will remove it from the list, but the updated information will be used the next time the product is used in a Pricing Action.

Displaying the Product Changes panel

The Product Changes panel is controlled by the system policy Suppress Price Change Log Panel. The default setting for this system policy is True (the Product Changes panel is hidden).

To display the Product Changes panel, a user with the appropriate permissions can:

  1. Navigate to Maintenance > Policies.
  2. Double-click on Module: Price Planning.
  3. Select Suppress Price Change Log Panel.
  4. Click the drop down arrow in the New Setting field and choose False.
  5. Click Apply Changes.
  6. Click Yes.
  7. Restart Demand Management.
