Create a Price Action

Within the Price Manager screen, click the Actions tab, next to Information, which will load the Price Action screen. Click New, enter the required fields and click Ok to create a new Pricing Plan.

Field descriptions

Field Description

Name (Required)

Enter a descriptive label to summarize the Pricing Action.

Description (Optional)

Enter a summary regarding the purpose of the Pricing Action.

Start Date (Required)

Select a future date that the Pricing Action will set new product pricing. The system will default and restrict to no earlier than the Plan Start Date.

Strategy (Required)

The Pricing Action Strategy will determine the fields to be used for user input, how the pricing will be calculated/validated based on the priority of Price Strategy Rules and the types of validation failed severities configured to determine pricing.

The Price Strategies available to be selected are filtered based on the Price Action Strategy’s restricted User and Role access.

Variant (Required)

Pricing Actions may be configured for a subset of products configured in the Pricing Plan. Additional Product Groups may be added to the Pricing Action only after being added to the Pricing Plan.

Site Clusters (Required)

Site Cluster Sets are available based on Site Clustering configuration.
