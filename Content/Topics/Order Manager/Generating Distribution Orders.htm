Generating Distribution Orders

Distribution orders move items from DCs to stores, DCs to DCs, or stores to stores.

Setup

The following objects should be set up before generating a distribution order:

  • A filter that includes the items and/or sites that you want to distribute.
  • The supply chain that you want to use.
  • Demand forecasts for the items you want to distribute.
  • Stocking strategies for the items you want to distribute.

Instructions

  1. From the Execute tab, click Order Manager.
  2. In the Order toolbar, click Distribution.

  3. In the Items dialog box, select the filter with the items and sites you want to distribute.

  4. In the Add Distribution Order dialog box, enter the parameters for the order.

  5. Click OK.

    Once Demand Management calculates the recommended orders, they appear in the Order screen. Each row represents a different recommended order, and contains basic details of the order.

  6. Double-click on an order row to view the demand forecast, stocking strategy, stock holding projection, and other details that drove the order recommendation.

Proceed according to your company's order authorization workflow.

Results

The order follows your company's authorization workflow. Once authorized, Demand Management sends the order to your ERP for processing.
