Scenario Detail Section

Review the actual items that were used in the calculation of each performance band in the Scenario Results section and selectively exclude items from the calculation.

Displaying the Scenario Detail section

To display the Scenario Detail section:

  • click the Scenario Detail widget located on the right hand side of the Rate of Sale Calculator section.

Undocking the Scenario Detail section

To undock the Scenario Detail section:

  1. Click on the Scenario Detail widget, right click on the heading and select Float.

    The Scenario Detail section can now be moved around and resized as per user preference.

Including or excluding items from a scenario

To include or exclude items:

  1. Check or uncheck the item’s check box in the Included field.

  2. Click Save.

    Once calculated, the results of the scenario with or without the selected items display.

  3. Recalculate the scenario by clicking on the Calculate button on the Rate of Sale Calculator section toolbar.
