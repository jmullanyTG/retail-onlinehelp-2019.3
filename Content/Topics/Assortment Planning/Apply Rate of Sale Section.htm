Apply Rate of Sale Section

In this section, the user applies the results of a Rate of Sale scenario to the placeholders/collections selected in the Main Planning view.  Upon applying the results, the sales plans for the selected placeholders/collections will be adjusted to reflect the ROS values that have been applied.

This section should already be populated based on the selected lines in the main planning view. If not, click Execute to update the view.

Applying rates of sale

To apply rates of sale:

  1. In the Scenario Results section, check the boxes corresponding to the rates of sale to be applied to the selected placeholder/variant/cluster combinations.

  2. In the Apply Rate of Sale section, check the Grand Total check box of the variants/placeholders or collections making them eligible for the ROS values selected in the Scenario Results section.
  3. Click Copy Rate of Sale at the top of the Apply Rate of Sale section to populate the selected ROS values from the Scenario Results section.

  4. Click Save to commit the chosen rates of sale to the selected placeholders/collections as shown in the Apply Rate of Sale section.

    Before clicking Save, users can clear any copied ROS values by clicking Undo on the Apply Rate of Sale toolbar.
