Metric Definitions

After the user has set the parameter selections at the top of the screen, clicking Execute returns the following metrics in the view for comparison of the two plans the user has selected.  The metrics defined below are the foundational metrics that are common between Assortment Planning and Merchandise Financial Planning:

Metric

 Definition
Total Sales

Sales revenue aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and/or merchandise financial planning
Total Sales Variance

The difference in Total Sales between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2
Total Sales Variance %

The percentage above or below total sales of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
Total Units

Sales units aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and/or merchandise financial planning
Total Units Variance

The difference in Total Units between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2
Total Units Variance %

The percentage above or below total units of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
COGS

Cost of Sales aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and/or merchandise financial planning
COGS Variance

The difference in COGS between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2 within the reconciliation report
COGS Variance %

The percentage above or below COGS of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
AUR

Average Unit Retail aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and/or merchandise financial planning

Calculation: (Total Sales/Total Units)
AUR Variance

The difference in AUR between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2 within the reconciliation report
AUR Variance %

The percentage above or below AUR of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
AUC

Average Unit Cost aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and/or merchandise financial planning

Calculation: (COGS/Total Units)
AUC Variance

The difference in AUC between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2 within the reconciliation report
AUC Variance %

The percentage above or below AUC of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
AUT

Average Unit Ticket aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and/or merchandise financial planning

Calculation: (Reg. Price Sales/Reg. Price Units)
AUT Variance

The difference in AUT between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2 within the reconciliation report.
AUT Variance %

The percentage above or below AUT of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
Margin

Profit margin expressed in currency value aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and/or merchandise financial planning

Calculation: (Total Sales – COGS)
Margin Variance

The difference in Margin between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2
Margin Variance %

The percentage above or below Margin of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
BOP

Beginning of Period inventory at cost aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and/or merchandise financial planning
BOP Variance

The difference in BOP at cost between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2
BOP Variance %

The percentage above or below BOP of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
BOP Units

Beginning of Period inventory units aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and merchandise financial planning
BOP Units Variance

The difference in BOP Units between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2
BOP Units Variance %

The percentage above or below BOP Units of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
EOP

End of Period inventory at cost aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and/or merchandise financial planning
EOP Variance

The difference in EOP at cost between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2
EOP Variance %

The percentage above or below EOP of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
EOP Units

End of Period inventory units aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and/or merchandise financial planning
EOP Units Variance

The difference in EOP Units between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2
EOP Units Variance %

The percentage above or below EOP Units of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
Receipts

Total intake or receipts at cost aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and/or merchandise financial planning
Receipts Variance

The difference in Receipts at cost between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2
Receipts Variance %

The percentage above or below Receipts at cost of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
Receipt Units

Total intake units or receipt units aggregated to the levels of the product and site hierarchies defined in assortment planning and/or merchandise financial planning
Receipt Units Variance

The difference in Receipt Units between Comparison Plan 1 and Comparison Plan 2
Receipt Units Variance %

The percentage above or below Receipt Units of Comparison Plan 1 versus Comparison Plan 2
