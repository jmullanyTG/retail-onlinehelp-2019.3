Assortment Rollup

Assortment plans typically contain granular data related to the item plans of an organization, as it represents the detail behind what items are being bought, when items deliver, and the lifecycle information for items related to Sales, Cost of Sales, Margin, Receipts, and Inventory ownership.  This information can be leveraged within an organization to view plans at various aggregated levels of the product, location and time hierarchies. 

The aggregation of assortment plan data at established levels of the product and location hierarchies over time provides insight to an organization and helps to guide the organization in decisions around merchandising, pricing and promotions, open to buy, profitability and inventory management.  The Assortment Rollup screen has been designed as a standard feature within the Assortment Planning Module in order for organizations to aggregate their assortment plan data to the established hierarchical levels regarding products and locations for review and analysis.

The Assortment Rollup contains all the foundational metrics related to the aggregation of sales, margin, receipt and inventory data up the product and location hierarchies defined in the active assortment plan.  Users have the ability to review future period plans and actualized historical data.   One of the key components of the Assortment Rollup lets users review plan vs. actualized data for periods within the active assortment plan by utilizing the comparison plan functionality.
