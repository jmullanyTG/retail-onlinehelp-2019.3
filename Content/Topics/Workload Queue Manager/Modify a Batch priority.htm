Modify a Batch priority

There may be a need to escalate or de-escalate a Batch’s priority. A Super User with access to the Workload Queue screen, with permissions to reprioritize, may modify a Batch that has not started its execution.

To modify a Batch’s priority:

  1. Click the priority drop down.
  2. Select the new appropriate Priority Type.
  3. Click Save to commit the modification.

Multiple Batches’ priority may be modified before clicking Save to commit the modification(s) which will cause the system to refresh the list with the updated priorities.

Escalation of a Batch’s priority will not cause a change to its Expiration Date, where as a de-escalation will.
