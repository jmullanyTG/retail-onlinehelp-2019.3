Workload Queue Priorities

  • Synchronous - Bypasses the Workload Queue and runs in parallel with any queued Batches.
  • Immediate - The Batch estimated workload was calculated to run first during the daily time frame, in parallel with Synchronous Batches.
  • ASAP - The Batch estimated workload was calculated to run during the daily time frame, after Immediate Batches, taking into account escalation based upon the Batch’s S-Curve time factor.
  • Low Priority - The Batch estimated workload was calculated to run during the daily time frame, after ASAP and Immediate Batches, taking into account escalation based upon the Batch’s S-Curve time factor.
  • Nightly - The Batch estimated workload was calculated to run during the nightly time frame, after Low Priority, ASAP and Immediate Batches, taking into account escalation based upon the Batch’s S-Curve time factor.
  • Weekend - The Batch estimated workload was calculated to run during the weekend time frame, after Nightly, Low Priority, ASAP and Immediate Batches, taking into account escalation based upon the Batch’s S-Curve time factor.
