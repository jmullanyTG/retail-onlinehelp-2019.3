Example - Defining Stocking Strategy and Store Eligibility

Policy tasks apply replenishment and stocking strategy-related settings to the items contained in the task's filter.

In this example, you will create the following policy tasks to support the introduction of the new item High Collar Angelina Jacket to stores:

  • A policy task that sets the default distribution center stocking strategy for the new item.
  • A policy task that sets the default store stocking strategy for the new item.
  • A policy task that excludes stores outside of the USA and Canada from receiving this item.

Workflow

Click to view the Managing Stocking Strategies workflow.

Instructions

1. Create a Policy Task for DCs
  1. From the Execute tab, click Policy. The Policy Task grid displays.
  2. From the Policy Task toolbar, click New. The Add Policy Task screen displays.

  3. In the Name field on the Global tab, enter New Items (to DC).

  4. From the Filter field, click Change and select the DC All Items - Stocking Strategy filter.

  5. On the Policy tab, enter the following values:

    • Order Policy Type: Normal Stocked
    • Review Period Days: 1
    • Replenishment Cycle Days: 30
    • Safety Stock Days: 21
  6. Click OK. The policy task is created and the displays on the Policy Task grid.
 2. Create a Policy Task for Stores
  1. From the Execute tab, click Policy. The Policy Task grid displays.
  2. From the Policy Task toolbar, click New. The Add Policy Task screen displays.

  3. In the Name field on the Global tab, enter New Management Store.

  4. From the Filter field, click Change and select the New Item Policy Management: Stores filter.

  5. On the Policy tab, enter the following values:

    • Order Policy Type: Normal Stocked
    • Review Period Days: 1
    • Replenishment Cycle Days: 7
    • Safety Stock Days: 14
    • Minimum Safety Stock Units: 2
  6. Click OK. The policy task is created and the displays on the Policy Task grid.
 3. Create a Store Eligibility Policy Task
  1. From the Execute tab, click Policy. The Policy Task grid displays.

  2. From the Policy Task toolbar, click New. The Add Policy Task screen displays.

  3. In the Name field on the Global tab, enter Store Eligibility: High Collar Angelina Jacket Only Active for Canada and USA.

  4. From the Filter field, click Change and select the High Collar Angelina Jacket Store Eligibility filter.

  5. In the Order Policy Type field on the Policy tab, enter Redundant.

  6. Click OK. The policy task is created and the displays on the Policy Task grid.
