Managing Notifications

From the Notification inbox, you can view, sort, and dismiss notifications.

You may dismiss notifications that are no longer relevant to remove them from immediate view. The dismissed notifications are stored under History on the Notification Inbox screen.

You can also move the Notification Inbox screen to another screens.

You can sort notification by time, solution, and type.

To Sort Notifications

  1. From any screen in Demand Management, click the Notification icon on the right side at the top of the screen. The Notification Inbox displays.
  2. From the Sort By drop-down list, select how you wish to sort your notifications: by Time, Solution, or Type.
  3. The notifications display in the selected sorted order.

To Dismiss a Notification from any Screen

  • When the notification displays on the screen, click the X icon. The notification is dismissed and disappears from the screen.

To Dismiss a Notification in the Notification Inbox

  1. From the Notification Inbox, do one of the following:
  • To dismiss all notifications at once, click Dismiss All. The notifications are dismissed and disappear from the Notification Inbox.
  • To dismiss a single notification, click the red icon next to the notification. The notification is dismissed and disappears from the Notification Inbox.

To View a Dismissed Notification

  • From the Notification Inbox, click History. The dismissed notifications display.
