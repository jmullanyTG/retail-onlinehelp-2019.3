Managing Custom Notifications

Only new notifications that are not on the History tab can be updated.

To Update a Custom Notification

  1. From the Maintenance solution, click Notification Maintenance. The Notification Maintenance screen displays.
  2. Select the custom notification you wish to update. The Notification Properties panel displays the notification details.
  3. Make the necessary changes.
  4. Click Save. The custom notification is updated.

To Delete a Custom Notification

  1. From the Notification Maintenance screen, select the custom notification you wish to delete. The Notification Properties panel displays the notification details.
  2. Click Delete. A confirmation message displays.
  3. Click OK. The custom notification is deleted.
  4. Click Save.
