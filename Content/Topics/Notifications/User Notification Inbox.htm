User Notification Inbox

Notifications inform you when processes (e.g. calculations, forecasts, validations, etc.) have been initiated, canceled, expired, failed, or completed and if any errors occurred. Notification delivery settings are system-configured, but you can change these settings on the Notification Preferences screen.

The notification icon also displays the number of unread notifications.

To Access the Notification Inbox

  • From any screen in Demand Management, click the flag icon on the right side at the top of the screen. The Notification Inbox displays.
