Managing Notification Subscription

The Notification Maintenance screen provides System Administrators the ability to subscribe users to receive solution notifications.

There are three notification solution areas on the Notification Maintenance screen:

  • Solution Subscriptions
  • Subscription Properties
  • Delivery Descriptions

You may subscribe to all or to a set of core and custom notifications, along with their defaults delivery method, for each Demand Management solution.

By default, the system marks any subscribed notification with a Soft delivery method. For details, see Notification Delivery Methods.

You can change the delivery method on the User Notification Preferences screen.

To Subscribe to Notifications

  1. From the Maintenance tab, click Notification Maintenance. The Notification Maintenance screen displays.
  2. Select the check boxes of the respective solution(s) you wish to subscribe to.
  3. Do one of the following:
  • To subscribe to all solutions, select the check box at the solution level, such as Allocations, Assortment, Inventory Forecast, etc.
  • To subscribe to individual solutions, select each of the respective solutions.
  1. Select the delivery method for the solutions you are subscribed to.
