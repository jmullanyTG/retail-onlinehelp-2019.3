Example - Stock Shortfall

Stock shortfall exceptions identify items that are projected to be understocked at a location, resulting in safety stock at that location being sold. If incoming orders already exist for these items, you may choose to expedite those orders to prevent or minimize the shortfalls. If no orders have yet been created for these items, you may choose to create expedited orders for them.

You can use filters and views to create ad-hoc Stock Shortfall reports in Inventory Manager. In this example, we'll use two pre-created filters. The first identifies items with 3 or more days of potential stock shortfalls. The second filter identifies items with 3 or more days of potential stock shortfalls which also have incoming orders that can be expedited to alleviate the shortfall.

Instructions

Stock shortfall exception
  1. From the Manage tab, click Inventory Manager.

  2. In the Items dialog box, select the Inventory Exception: Shortfall filter.

  3. In the Choose Fields dialog box, select the Inventory Exception: Shortfalll view.

  4. In the item document, you can see all of the items that exceed the Shortfall Stock Days threshold specified in the filter.

    For this example, the threshold is "more or equal to 3 days".

  5. In the grid, you can sort items by priority ("A" items listed first) and review each item's Shortfall Stock Days, Shortfall Stock Quantity, Shortfall Stock Value, and more.

  6. In the Details pane, review an item's Stock Holding Projection tab to see the projected shortfall (where projected stock on hand dips into the safety stock).

  7. Review other tabs in the Detail pane as desired to get a better understanding of the forecasted demand, inventory policy, and other information governing the item stock at the location.
 Stock shortfall exception (with expedite option)
  1. From the Manage tab, click Inventory Manager.

  2. In the Items dialog box, select the Inventory Exception: Shortfall Expedite filter.

  3. In the Choose Fields dialog box, select the Inventory Exception: Shortfall Expedite view.

  4. In the item document, you can see all of the items that exceed the Shortfall Stock Days threshold specified in the filter.

    For this example, the threshold is "more or equal to 60 days".

  5. In the grid, you can sort items by priority ("A" items listed first) and review each item's Shortfall Stock Days, Shortfall Stock Quantity, Shortfall Stock Value, and more.

  6. In the Details pane, review an item's Stock Holding Projection tab to see the projected shortfall (where projected stock on hand dips into the safety stock) that occurs before the incoming order arrives.

  7. Click on the Inbound and Outbound Orders tab in the Details pane to see the upcoming order details.

  8. Review other tabs in the Detail pane as desired to get a better understanding of the forecasted demand, inventory policy, and other information governing the item stock at the location.
