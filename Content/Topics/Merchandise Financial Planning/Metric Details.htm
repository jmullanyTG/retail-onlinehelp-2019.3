Metric Details

  • Comp Sales:  Sales coming from all locations that have been defined as comparative or open for more than 12 months.
    • Simple Calculation:   (Comp Sales Units * Comp Sales AUR)
  • LY Comp Sales:   Last year Sales coming from all current locations that have been defined as comparative or open for more than 12 months
    • Simple Calculation:   (LY Comp Sales Units * LY Comp Sales AUR)
  • Comp Sales % +/- LY:   Comp sales percent increase or decrease to comparative period last year
    • Simple Calculation:   (Comp Sales – LY Comp Sales)/(Comp Sales)
  • Comp Sales Units:   Sales Units coming from all locations that have been defined as comparative or open for more than 12 months
    • Simple Calculation:   (Comp Sales/Comp Sales AUR)
  • LY Comp Sales Units:   Last year Sales Units coming from all current locations that have been defined as comparative or open for more than 12 months
    • Simple Calculation:   (LY Comp Sales/LY Comp Sales AUR)
  • Comp Sales Units % +/- LY:   Comp Sales Units percent increase or decrease to comparative period last year
    • Simple Calculation: (Comp Sales Units – LY Comp Sales Units)/(Comp Sales Units)
  • Comp Sales AUR:   Average unit retail coming from all locations that have been defined as comparative or open for more than 12 months
    • Simple Calculation:   (Comp Sales/Comp Sales Units)
  • LY Comp Sales AUR:   Last year average unit retail coming from all current locations that have been defined as comparative or open for more than 12 months
    • Simple Calculation:   (LY Comp Sales/LY Comp Sales Units)
  • Non-Comp Sales:  Sales coming from all locations that have been defined as non-comparative or open for 12 months or less
    • Simple Calculation:   (Non-Comp Sales Units * Non-Comp Sales AUR)
  • LY Non-Comp Sales:   Last year Sales coming from all current locations that have been defined as non-comparative or open for 12 months or less
