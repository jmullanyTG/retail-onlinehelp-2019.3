Grid

The grid will display the actual data values for the forecast, demand and other series displayed in the graph. As such, the descriptions of the series are identical to those for the graph.

Series Description
Core
Total Sales Historical demand from sales history. Will match the imported history totals of sales for the inventory item.
Total Lost Sales If, when selecting forecast methods, users elect to include lost sales in their calculations, the lost sales totals will be represented by the purple bar.
Effective Total The effective total for demand history. Will determine the input to the forecasting engine when determining historical demand. The effective total series will usually run along the top of the total sales bar graph and total lost sales bar graph (if lost sales have been included).
Manual Forecast The manual forecast series is represented as a red or burgundy dot. Upon entering the he manual forecast series will overlap the effective forecast series and will only diverge upon manual manipulation of the series, either by dragging the dot in the graph, or the addition of a value to the manual forecast row in the grid. Once changes are made, the dot will change to burgundy to differentiate the series from the effective forecast which was displayed upon entering the viewer.

Sales The sales history for the period.
Lost Sales The lost sales calculated for the period.
LY Total Sales The total sales for this period last year.

Original Forecast When selecting between different forecast models, the yellow bar graph will show the resultant winner of the forecast tournament. This can be a useful comparison tool when choosing a forecast method.
