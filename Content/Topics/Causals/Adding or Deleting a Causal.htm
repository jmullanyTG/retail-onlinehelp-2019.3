Creating or Deleting Causals

Creation of new causals and linking is performed within the Causals module. However, the series values may also be edited within the Forecast Viewer. Deleting a causal may be done by selecting it from the drop down, then clicking the Delete button. Deletion eliminates all links.

Adding a new causal

  1. From the Execute tab, click Causals.
  2. In the Causals toolbar, click New to create a new causal.

  3. In the New dialog box, complete the fields as required and click Ok:

    The bucket type selected may be daily, weekly, or monthly; however, it is important that the bucket type selected match the forecasts bucket type.

    The new causal appears in the Causals screen.

  4. In the Series Values tab, enter values in each week that corresponds to the promotion.

  5. Click Save in the toolbar to see the results.

  6. In the Level Links tab, click Add in the Level toolbar to link the new causal to the filter with the items.

  7. Select a filter from the Filter field drop down menu and click Ok.

  8. The Level Links tab now displays the items that are linked to this causal.
