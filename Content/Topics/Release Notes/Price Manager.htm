Price Manager

The following enhancements are now available in the Price Manager solution.

Price & Markdown DMDEV-63634DMDEV-62714

Within a Pricing Plan, two new menu options have been added to the toolbar of the Sites grid. The options are labeled New Cluster and Add Site. Users now have the ability to create new Site Clusters (that must contain at least one Site) and to include additional Sites in existing Site Clusters. The new Site Clusters and additional sites can be applied to future Price Actions not already exported or in the process of being exported.

Restricting how users are able to generate new prices across a product portfolio DMDEV-61916 SEE DMDEV-65119

The system can now be configured using a new system policy labeled Product Pricing Level to restrict how users are able to generate new prices across a product portfolio to ensure down stream systems are sent data accurately.

There are three possible settings:

  • Variant\Site Pricing (Default)
  • Item\Site Pricing
  • Variant\Site and Item\Site Pricing

Setting the value to Variant\Site and Item\Site Pricing allows users to manipulate the generation of pricing at a high level (Variant\Site) as well as have greater control and degree of manipulation of pricing at a lower level (Item\Site). All Price Actions within a Pricing Plan will be limited to either Variant\Site or Item\Site pricing.
