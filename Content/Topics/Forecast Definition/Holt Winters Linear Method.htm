Holt-Winters Linear Method

This method is identical to the exponential smoothing with a trend method. There are different ways that the Demand Management forecasting engine processes these two methods, but they will likely yield the exact same results.

The Holt-Winters methodology is actually used in both exponential smoothing with a trend and exponential smoothing with a trend and seasonality. It is further utilized in the Holt-Winters additive trend additive seasonality method and the Holt-Winters multiplicative seasonality method.
