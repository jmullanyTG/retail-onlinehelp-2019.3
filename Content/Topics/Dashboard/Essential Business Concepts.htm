Essential Business Concepts

The standard dashboard will present four widgets, all of which are designed to give the user an overview of their inventory situation and highlight any exceptions which may require the attention of the user.

  • Inventory Status Over Time – A line graph showing several series related to the value of different stock types over a selected period of time.
    • Availability % - Percentage of items which are in stock vs. the items which should be in stock. i.e. a 90% availability will mean that 90% of the preferred stock is actually on hand.
    • Stock On Hand – The value of stock on hand.
    • Model Stock – The value of the preferred model stock.
    • Potential Stockout (POTSO) – The value of the items at risk of being out of stock.
    • Excess Stock – The aggregated value of stock overages at all sites.
  • Stock Mix – A pair of pie chart graphs depicting several measures broken down by contributions to both total item count, and perceived value.
    • Shortfall – The difference between on hand and safety stock, if items fall under the safety stock levels (may not appear if no items are under safety stock level.
    • Stock On Hand – The value and quantity of on hand items.
    • Model Stock – The value and quantity of the preferred model stock.
    • Potential Stockout (POTSO) – The value and quantity of the items at risk of being out of stock.
    • Excess Stock – The aggregated value and quantity of stock overages at all sites.
  • Inventory Status By Priority – A display of many important inventory metrics broken down by priority based on the Pareto classification of the items.
    • Item Count – Number of items in this classification.
    • Last Month Revenue – The associated revenue from the items in this classification in the previous month.
    • LY Revenue - The associated revenue from the items in this classification in the previous year.
    • Revenue Change – Positive or negative percentage representing the change in revenue.
    • Last Month Sales – Volume of items sold in this classification in the previous month.
    • Stock on Hand – Value of stock on hand for the items in this classification.
    • On Order – Value of the on order items in this classification.
    • Potential Stockout – Value of the items in this classification potentially at risk of stocking out.
  • Dashboard Action Items – A display of the quantity of items (and their value) which fall into the defined exception filters in addition to an “Action” button which will launch and Inventory Manager view.
    • Forecast Exceptions (Manual Forecasts) - These are items which have a manual forecast override in the near future.
    • Forecast Exceptions (Model Fit Error) - These are items with a low forecast model fit (MAPE).
    • On Order (Late) – Items which are late and it may be necessary to contact the supplier regarding the delivery schedule.
    • On Order (Normal) – Items which are on order, but within ordering exception tolerance.
    • On Order (Surplus) – Items which are on order but deemed to be surplus. It may be necessary to contact the supplier regarding the delivery schedule.
    • Recommended Order (Normal) – Items which should be ordered today.
    • Recommended Order (Urgent) – Items which should be ordered today and possibly expedited.
    • Stock Exception (Excess) – Items deemed to be in excess at certain sites and are candidates for redistribution.
