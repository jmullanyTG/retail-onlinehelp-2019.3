Example: Assigning Schedules to Sites

Schedules help Demand Management determine when orders need to be placed so that inventory can get to your sites in time to meet demand.

In Site Manager, you can assign multiple types of schedules to a site. In this example, you will assign a receiving schedule to a new store in Nashville.

To Assign a Schedule to a Site

  1. From the Management tab, click Site Manager. The Items window displays.

  2. From the Filter drop-down menu, select the Identify New Stores filter. The Site Manager Document displays the recently added sites: Calgary and Nashville.

  3. In the local Columns toolbar, click Choose to select the additional fields you want to appear in the site document. In this example, select the Receiving fields in the Site Schedules group.

  4. In the Site Manager Document, locate the row in the grid for Nashville. Click in its Receiving Schedule column and select the schedule US Retail Stores - Week Day Delivery Only.

  5. In the Site Manager Document toolbar, click Save. The schedule assignment is saved.
