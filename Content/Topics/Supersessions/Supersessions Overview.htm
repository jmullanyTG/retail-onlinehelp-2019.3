Supersession Overview

Forecasting Supsersessions

To forecast demand for a new item that has no sales history of its own, you must use the sales data of a similar, existing item to generate a demand forecast for the new item. Forecasting supersessions copy the month-to-month sales history of an existing item to the new item so that a demand forecast can be made for the new item. The new demand forecast can then be used to create purchase orders for the new item, ensuring your new products are delivered to the right stores at the right time in the right quantities.

Replenishment Supersessions

When it is time to replace an old item with its new replacement item (such as when a new model is available), you can use replenishment supersessions to gradually replenish or immediately replace the old item inventory with the new item.
