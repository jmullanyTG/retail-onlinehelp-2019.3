Configuring Demand Transference for Showroom Items

Showroom items those items that are sold at the showroom location, but not fulfilled elsewhere.

To Configure Showroom Settings

  1. From the Execute tab, click Policy. The Policy Task screen displays.
  2. Click Edit. The Update Policy task screen displays.
  3. From the Order Policy Type drop-down menu on the Policy section, select Showroom.
  4. Make the necessary changes in the remaining sections, as follows:
  • Minimum Safety Stock Units: the minimum number of units to display in the showroom.
  • Designated Fulfillment Site: the designate site as the fulfillment location for showroom sales.
  • Demand Transference Method: if showroom orders can be fulfilled from multiple locations, select Split Factor.
  1. When finished, click OK.
