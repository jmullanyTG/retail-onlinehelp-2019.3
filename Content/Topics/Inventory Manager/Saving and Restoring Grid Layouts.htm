Saving and Restoring Grid Layouts

In instances where users have constructed a report, they may wish to save the fields and layout for future use. This means when they are at the Choose Fields screen, they are able to quickly select a previously saved layout and easily get columns into the grid.

  1. First ensure that there are no filters active on your grid, check that there is no writing in the headers and also no items selected in the little funnels.

    If you save a layout with filters applied you will get the behavior below each time you open your layout.

  2. Right click anywhere on the data and select Save Layout.

  3. Give the Layout a meaningful name and then check or uncheck the default box depending on if you want this layout to be the default layout.

    Most of the time you will choose private unless you are creating a layout that other members on your team will use.
