Order Policy Types

All remaining policy settings are related to the replenishment strategy for the item or items in the filter. Replenishment is made at review period intervals.

If inventory levels are expected to drop below the safety stock level in the next lead time, it is recommended that replenishment values restore the inventory to its maximum level.

The settings available on the Policy section on the Update Policy Task screen display depending on the selected order policy type; not all policy settings display at one time.

The order policy type determines the status for the items in the filter and if the item must be stocked, reordered, or set to another specific type.

Inventory stocking and policies depend on four item policy types available from the Update Policy Task screen:

Active Items (expecting demand)

  • Normal Stocked Items: require a demand, inventory and replenishment
  • Bin Items: require replenishment when on-hand falls below the bin minimum inventory level (e.g., active low volume, sporadic items)
  • Non-Stocked Items: require a demand plan, no inventory plan, and no proactive replenishment plan (e.g., make/assemble-to-order items)

Inactive items (no longer expected to sell)

  • Redundant Items: require no demand, inventory, or replenishment
Do Not Set allows for non-order policy specific settings (order multiple) to be updated without disrupting the order policy driven behavior.

To Access the Update Policy Task Screen

  1. From the Execute tab, click Policy. The Policy Task screen displays.
  2. Click Edit. The Update Policy task screen displays.
