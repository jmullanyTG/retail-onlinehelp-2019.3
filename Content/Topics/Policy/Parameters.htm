Parameters

The parameters used to return data to the pivot grid will all be used to narrow the scope of inventories.

  • Filter – An inventory filter. If Item Master Hierarchy and Site Hierarchy parameters are left blank, all inventories within the filter will be returned to the grid.
  • Item Master Hierarchy – Segments of the product hierarchy may be selected to narrow the scope within the inventory filter selected in the “Filter” parameter.
  • Site Hierarchy - Segments of the location hierarchy may be selected to narrow the scope within the inventory filter selected in the “Filter” parameter.

Item Master Hierarchy and Site hierarchy parameters may be reset by selecting the “NULL” check box to the right of the drop-down menus.
