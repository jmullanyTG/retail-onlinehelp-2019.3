Inventory Policy Details

The inventory policy details mirror the policies available at the inventory level in the Inventory Manager. If you set up the details in the inventory policies, those details will be applied as default to all of the items in the filter.

Policy Task Actions

Two Automatic and Manual policy indicators can be set in a policy task. These indicators determine if a policy must be changed manually or by the system.

Manual Order Policy Indicator

The Manual Order Policy indicator is a specific setting policy indicator for items in an order. For example, if an item is set to Manual, the item will not be changed from a Normal Stocked item to a Bin item in the policy task. The Manual Order Policy Indicator can be set to Automatic or Manual.

Manual Policy Setting

You can set an item to Manual so that the policy (review period, replenishment cycle, and safety stock) is not overwritten, unintentionally, by policy tasks. From the Manual Policy Setting window, you can set all items to either Manual (policy will not be overwritten in policy task), or Automatic.

Showroom Policy Setting

Showroom items are items that are sold at the showroom location, but not fulfilled elsewhere.

Do Not Update

To prevent the Manual Order Policy indicator and the Manual Policy Setting from being updated, select Do not Update.

To Updated a Policy Task Action

  1. From the Execute tab, click Policy. The Policy Task screen displays.
  2. Click Edit. The Update Policy task screen displays.
  3. Click the Policy Tab. The policy task actions details display.
  4. Update the necessary information, and click OK.
